Key Reminders

Tretinoin Tips

Apply only to fully dry skin, 20–30 min after cleansing (buffering method). Start once per week and build up gradually. Use a pea-sized amount — more is not better.

Never Combine

  • Tretinoin + AHA/BHA
  • Tretinoin + Vitamin C
  • Tretinoin + Benzoyl Peroxide
  • Azelaic Acid + AHA/BHA same night

SPF Is Everything

Sun exposure worsens PIH significantly. Reapply every 2 hours outdoors. All actives in this routine increase sun sensitivity — skipping SPF negates your results.

PanOxyl Caution

10% BP is high. Watch for dryness or new dark spots from irritation. If this occurs, reduce to once per week or switch to a 2.5–4% formula.

Always patch test new products · Consult a dermatologist specializing in skin of color for prescription guidance
